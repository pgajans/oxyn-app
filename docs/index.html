FAQ

Questions, answered

If something's not here, just drop us a line.

Is Oxyn really free?
Yes. The core experience — battery health, storage cleanup, one-tap optimize, daily tech news, and trivia — is completely free, forever. Premium is an optional upgrade with no forced trial, no credit card to sign up, and you can cancel any time.
Does it work offline?
Yes. All core analysis — battery health, storage scanning, junk detection, optimize — runs on your device and works without an internet connection. Only optional content (Daily Tech News, Tech Trivia, AI Doctor reports) needs a connection.
What data does Oxyn collect?
Only the minimum needed to keep the app working and stable. That means anonymous crash diagnostics and a handful of basic, non-personal usage counters. We never collect your contacts, photos, messages, location, or any identifiable personal information. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
Does it really improve battery life?
Oxyn doesn't pretend to magically extend battery capacity — no app can rewrite physics. What it does is help you build healthier charging habits (like the 80% smart charge alarm), monitor temperature to avoid heat damage, and stop the patterns that wear batteries out faster. Over months, that makes a real difference in how long your battery lasts.
How is Oxyn different from other cleaner apps?
Three big differences: (1) Zero tracking SDKs — most cleaner apps embed 10–20 third-party trackers; we use zero. (2) No fake "boost" placebos — we show real numbers from real system APIs. (3) A genuine free tier, not a 3-day trial dressed up as free. We make money from optional Premium, not from your data.
Is there an Android version?
Yes. Oxyn is built with Flutter for both iOS and Android — the same beautiful experience on both platforms. The Android version is currently in open beta on Google Play; full Production release is planned alongside the iOS launch. Email us if you'd like to join the Android beta.
Which languages does Oxyn support?
Five at launch: English, Turkish, Spanish, Portuguese, and Arabic. Oxyn automatically picks the right language based on your system settings, and you can switch any time from Settings.
Does Oxyn run on iPad?
Yes. Oxyn is a Universal app — the same purchase works on both iPhone and iPad. Premium subscriptions are linked to your Apple ID and sync across all your devices automatically.
What happens if I cancel Premium?
You keep Premium until your billing period ends. After that, the app falls back to the Free tier — you still have battery health, basic storage tools, one-tap optimize, and daily content. Premium features (full AI Doctor, ad-free) simply pause until you re-subscribe.
How can I contact support?
Email support@oxynapp.net — a real human reads every message, typically replying within 24–48 hours. You can also visit our Support page for help articles.
